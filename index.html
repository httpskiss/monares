About Paws

Mission

PAWS is dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating stray cats, ensuring they find safe and loving homes.

Vision

PAWS envisions a world where every stray and abandoned animal has a place to call home. The simplicity and elegance of the logo reflect the organization's vision of fostering a compassionate community where animal welfare is a priority.
